Data Management and Analytics using Business Intelligence Tool

Company Profile: Leading Jewelers in US.

Technology: Qlik Sense and QlikView are two powerful data analytics and visualization tools developed by Qlik, designed to empower organizations with data-driven insights. QlikView, the older of the two, is known for its guided analytics capabilities, allowing users to create interactive dashboards with predefined paths for exploring data. It is especially suited for organizations that need structured analytics solutions with less emphasis on ad-hoc exploration. On the other hand, Qlik Sense offers a more modern approach, focusing on self-service and data discovery, allowing users to interactively explore data with an intuitive drag-and-drop interface. Built on Qlik’s associative engine, Qlik Sense empowers users to make real-time, informed decisions, making it ideal for dynamic and flexible business environments where quick insights are crucial. Both tools provide robust, scalable solutions for data analytics, with Qlik Sense being the preferred choice for businesses looking to empower users with greater autonomy in exploring data.
